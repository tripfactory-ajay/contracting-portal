What is the ORN Europe Regional Office?
A fully ORN-owned and operated regional headquarters, not a third-party DMC partnership — the definitive model for ORN's global expansion
🏠
ORN-Owned & Operated
This is not a DMC partnership. ORN Europe is a fully owned regional office, staffed, structured, and managed entirely by ORN Group. Ajay Kawa as COO Europe leads all operations, reporting directly to Raj Kumar COO Global. Every decision, every hire, every contract sits within ORN Group.
✈️
All Inbound — All Global Offices
Every inbound passenger arriving into the UK or Europe from any ORN global office is managed entirely by ORN Europe. Transfers, tours, accommodation, itinerary execution, customer queries in destination, emergency support — all under one roof, all under ORN control.
🏫
Full EU DMC Oversight
ORN Europe directs, contracts, and quality-controls all EU DMC partners across every European destination. We do not merely coordinate — we set standards, audit performance, manage contracts, and have the authority to appoint or terminate EU DMCs on behalf of ORN Group.
📝
All EU Contracting
ORN Europe holds and manages all supplier contracts across the UK and Europe on behalf of ORN Group: hotels, transfer operators, guides, attraction tickets, MICE venues, airlines, and cruise ports. No other ORN office contracts independently in this region.
👥
Hiring & HR — ORN EU
ORN Europe manages all hiring, HR, structure, and team-building for the UK and EU regional operation. Ajay Kawa holds full authority over headcount decisions, role structures, compensation frameworks, and team development across the ORN Europe team.
🌎
The Global Blueprint
The ORN Europe model is the prototype for how ORN Group will build owned regional offices in other key destinations worldwide. Everything developed here — structures, policies, operational playbooks, commercial frameworks — becomes the foundation for future ORN regional offices.

The 90/10 Commercial Model
ORN Europe retains 90% of all revenue generated across the region — reflecting full ownership, operational responsibility, and investment in the regional infrastructure

90% to ORN Europe — on every booking across the region

As a fully owned ORN regional office rather than a third-party DMC partner, ORN Europe retains 90% of the Gross Profit on all bookings processed through the regional operation. The 10% retained by ORN Group covers global systems, technology, central marketing, and group-level support.

This model applies to all inbound bookings from all ORN global offices, all outbound sales generated by the UK/EU office, all MICE and group programmes, and all supplier margin generated through ORN Europe's contracting function.

📅
Revenue Statements
ORN Group issues regional revenue statements to ORN Europe on the 14th and 29th of each month, covering all bookings confirmed and completed in that period. Statements break down by booking source (originating ORN office), destination, product type, and Gross Profit.
💸
Revenue Payments
ORN Europe's 90% share is credited to the regional P&L account on the 15th and last working day of each month. The regional P&L is maintained in GBP. Currency conversion for bookings originating from non-GBP ORN offices uses the mid-market rate on the date of booking confirmation.
📊
Regional P&L Ownership
COO Europe owns the full regional P&L. This includes revenue from all inbound bookings, contracting margin generated through supplier negotiations, any outbound sales generated in market, and all ancillary revenue. COO Europe reports P&L performance to COO Global on a monthly basis.
💡 Gross Profit Definition

Gross Profit is the difference between the selling price charged to the customer (as per the originating ORN office's agreed rate) and ORN Europe's direct cost of delivering the service in destination. The 90/10 split applies to this margin. Where ORN Europe has negotiated supplier rates below the prevailing contracted rate, the additional margin is retained 100% within the regional P&L as a contracting efficiency benefit.

EU DMC Network
ORN Europe appoints, contracts, and manages all EU DMC partners — this is an ORN Group function, not delegated to individual ORN offices

ORN Europe holds full authority to appoint, direct, and terminate EU DMC partners on behalf of ORN Group. No individual ORN global office may contract directly with or appoint a DMC within the UK and EU region. EU DMC Appointment & Management Framework
How ORN Europe selects, onboards, and manages EU DMC partners
🔍 Appointment Criteria
EU DMC partners must demonstrate: minimum 5 years' inbound operating experience; existing hotel and supplier relationships in destination; English-speaking management; experience with Indian outbound travellers; financial stability; required insurance cover (USD 2M public liability minimum).
📊 Performance Management
All EU DMCs are subject to quarterly performance reviews with COO Europe or Head of EU DMC Management. Review covers: service quality scores, booking response times, complaint rate, rate competitiveness, and product intelligence contribution. Performance below standard triggers a 60-day improvement plan.
⛔ Termination Authority
ORN Europe holds full authority to terminate EU DMC partnerships without requiring approval from any individual ORN global office. Termination with cause: immediate. Termination without cause: 90 days' written notice. No EU DMC may be appointed or terminated by any other ORN entity without COO Europe sign-off.

Consequences of SLA Failure
ORN Europe's authority to act on SLA breaches by EU DMC partners
🔒
Booking Failures
Failure by an EU DMC to confirm a booking within 24 hours: ORN Europe may source services from an alternative provider at the EU DMC's cost. Persistent failures: Performance Notice issued with 60-day improvement plan or immediate contract review.
📊
Reporting Failures
Missing quarterly product intelligence reports: ORN Europe may reduce the EU DMC's agreed margin by 10% per missing inspection, up to 40% maximum for that quarter. Persistent reporting failures constitute grounds for contract termination.
⛔
Service Quality Failures
Sustained service quality below ORN Europe's minimum threshold: Performance Notice issued. If unresolved within 60 days, ORN Europe may terminate the EU DMC contract with 30 days' notice and appoint an alternative partner — no ORN global office approval required.
